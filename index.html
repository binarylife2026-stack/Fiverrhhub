Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about hiring through SkillHub.

How do you verify your freelancers? ▼

We conduct rigorous screening including portfolio review, skill testing, ID verification, and continuous performance monitoring. Only 5% of applicants make it to our platform.

Is my payment safe? ▼

Absolutely! All payments are processed through Fiverr's secure escrow system. Funds are only released to the freelancer after you approve the deliverables. If you're not satisfied, you're protected by their money-back guarantee.

What if the freelancer doesn't deliver? ▼

We have a 99.2% success rate. In the rare case of issues, our team mediates to ensure you receive quality work or a full refund. Plus, all projects are covered by Fiverr's buyer protection.

How quickly can I start? ▼

Most experts respond within 1 hour and can start within 24 hours of order confirmation. Simply browse profiles, select your expert, and initiate your project today.

Do you offer refunds? ▼

Yes! If you're not completely satisfied with the deliverable, you can request revisions or a refund through Fiverr's resolution center. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Can I hire multiple freelancers? ▼

Absolutely! Many clients hire multiple experts for different projects simultaneously. We offer volume discounts for clients who maintain ongoing relationships with our verified network.
